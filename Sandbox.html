Current Focus

Building out the core game map and realistic starter garage. We're making steady progress on creating a map layout that blends different car culture elements, from city streets to countryside roads.

Map Overview

The map will include various areas like:

  • Condensed LA-style city with tight streets and an urban feel
  • Japanese-inspired countryside roads (touge) for drifting
  • A dedicated drift track and a drag strip for racing
  • Dynamic scenery including sunsets, weather, and traffic

We aim for a map as expansive as GTA V, filled with realistic props like gas stations, breakable objects, animals, and more!

Garage

Start in a humble, rented garage behind a townhouse. This is where players will begin their journey before upgrading their space and cars.

Visual Goals

We’re aiming for high-end graphics with ray tracing and realistic terrain, inspired by games like GTA 6 and Forza. The goal is to create a visually stunning experience with realistic lighting, vegetation, and weather effects.

The game will feature detailed environments, including:

  • Realistic terrain for racing
  • Dynamic weather and time of day
  • Detailed vehicle models and modifications
